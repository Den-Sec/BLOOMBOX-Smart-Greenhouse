## Pinout List for BLOOMBOX Smart-Greenhouse

### MLX90614 (Thermometer)
- **SDA**: Pin 21
- **SCL**: Pin 22

### Real-Time Clock (RTC) Module
- **SDA**: Pin 21
- **SCL**: Pin 22

### DHT11 Temperature and Humidity Sensor
- **Data Pin**: Pin 0

### SD Card Module
- **MOSI**: Pin 23
- **MISO**: Pin 19
- **CLK**: Pin 18
- **CS (Chip Select)**: Pin 13

### Water Level Sensor
- **Pin 1**: Pin 35
- **Power Pin**: Pin 32

### Soil Moisture Sensor
- **Signal Pin**: Pin 34

### Water Pump Module
- **Ground Pin**: Relay 1

### Grow Light
- **Ground Pin**: Realy 2

### Fan 1
- **Ground Pin**: Realy 3

### Fan 2
- **Ground Pin**: Realy 4
  
### Relay Modules
- **Relay 1**: Pin 4
- **Relay 2**: Pin 16
- **Relay 3**: Pin 17
- **Relay 4**: Pin 5
